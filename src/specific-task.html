Task 42

Scaling up EV Markets & EV City Casebook

Objective

The number of electric vehicles on-road in China continues to grow, increasing by more than 1 million in 2019. in 2019, the number of NEVs on-road reached 3.81 million. Among them, the number of battery electric vehicles acocunted for 81%, reaching 3.1 million. The hybrid vehicles on the road was 1.08 million. FCV is currently in its initial stage of development, mainly for demonstrative operation, with only about 4000 vehicles on road, of which about 60% are logistic trucks.

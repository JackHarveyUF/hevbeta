View All Countries View All Tasks

Collaborations

The HEV TCP member countries collaborate on shared projects (Tasks) to better understand and address EV deployment challenges and provide guidance to policy makers.

Tasks

The actual work on hybrid and electric vehicles is done through a variety of different Tasks that are focused on specific topics.

Reports

The HEV TCP publish an annual report in the Spring each year, summarising country developments and Task updates.

Bi-Annual Meetings

The HEV TCP ExCo meets twice a year to discuss and plan the working programme.

The HEV TCP was formed in 1993 to produce and disseminate information about advanced electric, hybrid, and fuel cell vehicles
